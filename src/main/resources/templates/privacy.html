We respect your privacy. These guidelines explain how VideoHub handles your data and how you can stay in control of your personal information.

  1. 1 Keep your account secure. Don’t share your login details with anyone and choose a strong password.
  2. 2 We only collect the information we need to provide our services—such as your name, email, and videos you upload or watch.
  3. 3 You can update or delete your profile information at any time through your account settings.
  4. 4 Your videos stay yours. We only ask for permission to store and show them on VideoHub.
  5. 5 We never sell your information. If we partner with tools that analyze usage, we make sure they handle your data securely.
